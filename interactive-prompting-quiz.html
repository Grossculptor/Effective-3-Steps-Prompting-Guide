Effective 3 Steps Prompting Guide

Introduction

Welcome to the Effective Prompting Guide! This interactive presentation will teach you how to create better prompts for AI assistants.   Let's start with some key principles.

Lesson 1: Be Clear and Specific

When crafting prompts, it's crucial to be clear and specific about your task or question. This helps the AI understand your needs better.

Example:

Bad: "Help me with a presentation."

Good: "I need help creating a 10-slide presentation for our quarterly sales meeting. The presentation should cover our Q2 sales performance, top-selling products, and sales targets for Q3. Please provide an outline with key points for each slide."

Lesson 2: Use Examples

Providing examples of the kind of output you're looking for can greatly improve the AI's understanding of your needs.

Example:

Bad: "Write a professional email."

Good: "I need to write a professional email to a client about a project delay. Here's a similar email I've sent before: [Include example email]"

Lesson 3: Encourage Thinking

For complex tasks, asking the AI to "think step-by-step" or "explain your reasoning" can lead to more accurate and detailed responses.

Example:

Bad: "How can I improve team productivity?"

Good: "I'm looking to improve my team's productivity. Think through this step-by-step, considering factors like current productivity blockers, potential solutions, implementation challenges, and methods to measure improvement. For each step, please provide a brief explanation of your reasoning."
